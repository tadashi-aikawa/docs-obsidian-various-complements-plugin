## Definitions

Exclude internal links that point to non-existing files from the suggestions.

- `ON`
- `OFF` (default)

## Examples

### ON

Do not display unresolved internal links such as `[[Phantom note]]` as suggestions.

### OFF

Display both existing internal links and unresolved internal links as suggestions.
